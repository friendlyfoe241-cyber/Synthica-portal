About the Journal

A rigorous, open record for student research

Synthica Journal exists so that high-school and early-career student research gets the same rigor, discoverability, and credibility as published professional science. We follow IEEE publication ethics, COPE guidelines, and Society for Science safeguards — published immediately under CC BY 4.0 with a registered DOI and Google Scholar metadata.

01

Double-blind peer review

Two independent reviewers, written editorial rationale, and a four-week target turnaround on every submission.

02

IEEE-aligned ethics

Publication ethics, conflict-of-interest disclosure, authorship standards, and an AI-use policy modeled on IEEE.

03

Fully open access

Immediate CC BY 4.0 publication. No charges to authors. No charges to readers. Authors retain copyright.

04

Scholar-ready pages

Citation metadata, stable DOI URLs, crawlable HTML abstracts, and downloadable PDFs on every article page.

For Authors

From submission to publication

We accept original research (IMRaD), review articles, methods papers, perspectives, and commentaries. Authors must meet ethics, formatting, and disclosure requirements before peer review begins.

01

Pre-submission check

Verify originality, ethics compliance, formatting, and authorship criteria before submitting your manuscript.

02

Desk review

The Editorial Chair evaluates scope, format, and completeness within five business days.

03

Double-blind peer review

Two independent reviewers assess methodology, clarity, and contribution. Target turnaround: four weeks.

04

Publication

Accepted articles publish as HTML and PDF with a DOI, CC BY 4.0 license, and full citation metadata.

Editorial Policies

Policies built for trust

Our editorial policies follow IEEE publication ethics, COPE guidelines, and Society for Science safeguards. Authorship, AI disclosure, and human-subjects research are treated with the same seriousness as professional venues.

01

Peer Review

Double-blind, minimum two reviewers, conflict recusal, written rationale on every editorial decision.

02

Open Access

Immediate CC BY 4.0 publication. No article-processing charges. Authors retain copyright.

03

Authorship & CRediT

All authors meet ICMJE criteria. CRediT taxonomy documents individual contributions on every article.

04

AI Disclosure

AI tools cannot be authors. Substantive AI-generated content must be disclosed in line with IEEE policy.

05

Publication Ethics

No plagiarism, fabrication, falsification, or simultaneous submission. Ethics approval required for human-subjects work.

06

Corrections & Retractions

Factual errors corrected via published Erratum. Research fraud results in retraction with a permanent visible notice.

Submit

Submit your manuscript

A credible, citable, open-access venue for student STEM research — at zero cost to authors or readers. Submissions are reviewed in the order received.

Before you submit

  • 150–250 word structured abstract (Background, Methods, Results, Conclusion)
  • 3–8 semicolon-separated keywords
  • IEEE-style numbered references
  • Originality and authorship declaration signed by the corresponding author
  • Ethics documentation for any human-subjects research
  • AI use disclosure where tools materially affected the manuscript
