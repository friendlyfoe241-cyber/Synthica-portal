SISTER Program

Students in STEM Through Engagement & Research — expanding access, mentorship, and community for the next generation of scientists.

About SISTER

STEM access with purpose

The SISTER Program is Synthica's initiative to lower barriers for students exploring STEM research. Through peer networks, guided workshops, and connections to mentors, participants build confidence and skills before diving into advanced projects.

Community & peers

Connect with students worldwide who share your interests in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

  • ✓ Discord study channels
  • ✓ Regional ambassador support
  • ✓ Peer accountability groups

Workshops & seminars

Live and async sessions on research literacy, careers in STEM, and how to get started without prior experience.

  • ✓ Research fundamentals
  • ✓ Guest speakers from academia
  • ✓ Q&A with mentors

Pathway to research

Graduates of SISTER activities are encouraged to apply for the Research Group, Free Course, and Global Research Challenge.

  • ✓ Research Group applications
  • ✓ Competition preparation
